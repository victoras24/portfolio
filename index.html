About me

Hey! My name is Victoras and I'm based in Cyprus. After working as an accountant for 3 years, I discovered my true passion in data analysis. I am fascinated by the ability to quantify reality and the power of data to reveal meaningful insights. I see myself as a detective, constantly seeking clues in the information gathered and using my skills to process and present it in a way that is easily accessible to end-users. My expertise lies in transforming raw data into meaningful insights that can drive decision-making and progress. I am dedicated to making the most of every piece of information and presenting it in a way that is not only informative, but also visually appealing. Effective communication of data is just as important to me as the analysis itself, and I strive to excel in both.
